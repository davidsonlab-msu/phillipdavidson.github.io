Evolution of gene expression, development, and genomes.

Phillip Davidson, Ph.D.
NSF Postdoctoral Fellow @ Indiana University

Welcome! I research how diversity arises from the evolution of genomes and developmental processes. I use bioinformatic and developmental approaches to better understand the evolution of genes, their expression, the networks they compose, and the bodies they construct. My primary focus is centered on the evolution of regulatory elements and their contribution to trait evolution and adaptation.
